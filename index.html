about us

Trueman Sundeck was Established in 2012, we have been working hard over the years to make properties look great and enjoyable with our quality installations. Our choice of high quality timber and our skilled workmanship has helped us to remain the number one choice for many of our customers in and around KZN.

Trueman Sundeck is dedicated to timber decking, solid wood flooring and associated pool decks, patio decking, sun decks and in fact any wooden or timber decking variation, including lofts and mezzanine floors.

We add a sophisticated touch to homes by installing beautiful, eye-catching yet structurally sound decks. We offer a variety of Cladding options using composite cladding in variety of forms to bring stylish touches to your property. With the look of natural wood, our composite balustrades complement the natural surroundings of your garden and home beautifully. We can add a final touch by installing beautiful custom made Pergolas which can be extended from the patio.

Decking Timber Choices

SA Timber


An great looking timber for outside deck installations. Extra care needed when sealing. Specially ordered H4 treated.

Balau Timber


Native to Malaysia,very tough and naturally toxic to termites make this wood hard to beat! Seal or leave to weather.

Garapa Timber


This timber is hard and tough perfect for the S.A. climate.Can be overcoated or left to weather. Clear coat is best!
